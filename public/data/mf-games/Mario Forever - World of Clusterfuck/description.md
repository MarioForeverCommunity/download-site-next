Ever wanted an MF Fangame filled with literally everything ? Clusterfuck answers that question !
World of Clusterfuck, alongside its sequel in the game, is a series of random levels with a
consistent story. Mostly inspired of Mushroom Kingdom Fusion.

There are 33 levels in total, most of them from WoC 1 and 2, while the rest are side levels and
secret levels, will you be able to find them all ?

The difficulty is a rollercoaster from Normal, Hard to Harder, test your skills !
