# Mario Forever: Advanced Mario Bros.

**Mario Forever: Advanced Mario Bros.** is a fan game developed by **AppliconReal** with **Cloud Engine 2.2** which takes place four years after the events of **Mario Forever**. It aims to feel close to the original game while providing new challenges, and maintains one important characteristic of Mario Forever — **simplicity**. A few people may recognize it from its Mario Worker Remake prototype.

**Developing & Level Designing:** AppliconReal

**Plot:** Four years after Mario's victory against Bowser, Toadstool is yet again invaded by Bowser. Princess Peach has been kidnapped, and the entirety of Toadstool is bombarded with chaos and disaster. However, without a second thought, Mario volunteers to defeat Bowser in battle to save Toadstool from the dark age once again. His brother, Luigi, too; couldn't just sit back and watch. And so, he decides to make his own ways in defeating Bowser, agreeing to meet Mario some time later so that they can pursue their heroic goals together.

**Number of Levels:**
**30** in total; **15** in the demo version.

- World 1: **5**
- World 2: **4**
- World 3: **5**
- World 4: **4**
- World 5: **6** (5 main + 1 optional level)
- World 6: **6** (5 main + 1 optional level)

**Themes**:
- World 1: Overworld / Waterland
- World 2: Starland
- World 3: Skyland
- World 4: Fastrun / Afternoon
- World 5: Pipeland
- World 6: Darkland

**Difficulty:** Generally leaning towards difficult.

## Features

- **Continue System:** Players have the option to choose between playing with or without continues. This is togglable in the settings and is on by default.
- **Remixed Soundtracks:** Some level soundtracks have their separate covers/remixes. You can choose to listen to them or toggle it off in the settings to listen to the original songs. This is currently a _WIP_, hence why many soundtracks still don't have their remixed versions. In the future, official music packs may also be provided.
- **Freestyle:** You can choose to play a specific world without having to start the entire game over through a section in the _Save Game Room_. Future updates may allow you to choose specific levels as well.
