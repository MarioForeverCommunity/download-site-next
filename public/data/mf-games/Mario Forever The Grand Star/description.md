**The Grand Star** has given its power everyone in the mushroom kingdom for ages. But prophecies tell that if it was to be stolen, the life of beings powered by it would wither away...

After the Grand Star is stolen by the King Koopa, its up to Mario and Twink to recover it, before its power runs out!

This is **Mario Forever: The Grand Star**, a Work In Progress fangame made in the **Thunder Engine**, which will have **32 levels across 8 worlds**, each 4th level having its own **special bossfight!**

**Worlds:** 2 (8)
**Levels:** 8 (32)

**Difficulty:** Mildly Difficult (?)

**Progress:** The game is currently in it's second demo as of November 3rd, 2025. More will be released as I develop the game.

**Engine:** Made in Thunder Engine.

**Resolutions:** 853x480 - 640x480
